Introduction

I started my journey into the world of Computer Science on May 14, 2016. I had just finished all requirements for senior year and I decided to try and learn something new with all of my spare time. At first, I thought programming was only for the brightest minds of the world so I didn’t expect to learn much or even enjoy it. Then I stumbled upon a fun website codeacademy.com and I took a couple of their courses and the rest is history. I am currently a Computer Science major in my second year and I love it. I am also an Economics minor because I feel that a lot of Computer Scientists need entrepreneurial skills and I enjoy learning about businesses and the economy. I attend Belmont University in Nashville,Tennessee.
